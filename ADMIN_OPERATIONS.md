# 🛡️ Admin Operations: Governance & Trusted Returns

This document outlines the professional procedures for managing interaction requests on the LOFO platform.

---

## 📋 Reviewing Interaction Requests

Admins must review every request to ensure the highest level of security and integrity.

### 🍱 Case A: "Submit a Claim" (Reclaiming Found Items)
- **Goal**: Verify that the claimant is the rightful owner of an item someone else found.
- **Verification Steps**:
  - Compare the **Description** vs. the **Private Details** provided by the finder.
  - Review **Proof Images** (Receipts, photos of the owner with the item, or unique identifiers).
  - Check for **Consistency** (Does the date/location reported by the claimant align with the found item?).

### 🍱 Case B: "Report Found" (Returning Lost Items)
- **Goal**: Verify that the person reporting they found an item actually has it in their possession.
- **Verification Steps**:
  - **MANDATORY**: A clear photo of the item you found.
  - Check **Context**: Does the found location match where the owner said it was lost?
  - **Logic**: Only approve once you are confident the item exists and is safe to return.

---

## 🔐 "Zero-Leak" Security Model

The LOFO platform uses a **One-Way Contact Sharing** model to protect users from scams and identity leaks.

- **Admin Mediation**: No contact details are shared upon submission.
- **One-Way Disclosure**:
  - Upon Approval: The **Poster** (the person who needs the item back OR the person holding the found item) receives the **Responder’s** contact info.
  - The **Responder** remains anonymous until the **Poster** chooses to reach out.
- **Admin Role**: Admins are the "Trust Bridge." Your approval is the trigger for this secure disclosure.

---

## 🚀 Resolving Items
- **Status Update**: Once a request is approved, the item is automatically marked as **`resolved`**.
- **Admin Notes**: Always provide clear instructions in the "Admin Note" field (e.g., "Meet at the Campus Security office" or "Contact the finder via their WhatsApp provided").
- **Audit Trail**: Every action is logged with your Admin ID and timestamp.
